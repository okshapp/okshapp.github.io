While BMI is a quick indicator of weight relative to height, it doesn’t fully capture individual health or disease risk. Factors like muscle mass, fat distribution, and lifestyle are essential to understanding true health, and BMI alone can overlook these crucial elements. For a more accurate assessment of your health, it’s always best to consult a medical expert, who can evaluate a range of indicators and provide personalized insights.
